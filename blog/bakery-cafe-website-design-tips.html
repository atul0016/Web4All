5 Ways a Website Can Double Your Bakery or Cafe's Customers

By SA-Flow • April 13, 2026 • 5 min read

You pour your heart into every pastry, every cup of coffee. Your regulars love you. But here's the thing — for every customer who walks in, there are 10 more who searched "best bakery near me" on Google and went to your competitor because they had a website and you didn't.

1. An Online Menu That Sells Before They Visit

When someone is deciding between two bakeries, the one with an online menu wins every time. Customers want to browse cakes, check prices, and see photos before they drive across town.

A website lets you showcase your best creations with mouth-watering images. Think of it as your display counter — but visible to everyone in the city, 24 hours a day.

Pro tip: Include prices on your menu. 73% of customers say they're more likely to visit a bakery that publishes prices online.

2. "Bakery Near Me" — The Search That Makes or Breaks You

Google processes over 8.5 billion searches per day. A huge chunk of those are local searches like:

  • "bakery near me"
  • "best cakes in Ranchi"
  • "birthday cake delivery Ranchi"
  • "cafe with WiFi near Morabadi"

Without a website, you're invisible in these results. With a website optimized for these keywords, you appear right when someone is ready to buy. This is the highest-intent traffic possible — people actively looking for what you sell.

3. WhatsApp Ordering That's Always Open

Here's a game-changer: add a WhatsApp button to your website. Customers browse your menu, tap "Order on WhatsApp," and message you directly. No app to download, no complicated cart system.

This works incredibly well for:

  • Custom cake orders for birthdays and weddings
  • Bulk orders for office parties
  • Daily bread and pastry pre-orders
  • Festival special orders (Diwali sweets, Christmas cakes)

Our Medium plan (₹2,500) includes WhatsApp integration built right in.

4. Google Reviews + Website = Trust Machine

When your bakery has a Google Business Profile linked to your website, customers can leave reviews that show up in search results. A bakery with 50+ positive reviews and a professional website is practically guaranteed to get new customers every week.

Your website becomes the landing page for those reviews — visitors see your menu, your photos, your location, and your 4.8-star rating all in one place.

5. Festival & Seasonal Promotions That Actually Reach People

Diwali sweets, Christmas cakes, Valentine's Day specials, Holi treats — every bakery and cafe has seasonal offerings. But how do you let people know about them?

With a website, you can:

  • Update your menu with seasonal specials
  • Create a special offers page that ranks on Google
  • Share your website link on social media, WhatsApp groups, and local communities
  • Collect pre-orders for bulk festival orders

Instagram posts disappear in the algorithm. A webpage stays indexed on Google forever.

Real Numbers: What a Website Can Do

Let's do simple math. Say your bakery averages 30 walk-in customers per day. With a website:

  • Google search visibility brings 5-10 new customers per week
  • WhatsApp orders add 3-5 orders per week
  • Festival promotions can double weekly orders during peak seasons

That's 30-40+ additional customers per month, growing over time as your Google ranking improves. All from a one-time investment of ₹1,500 to ₹5,000.

What Should a Bakery Website Include?

At minimum, your bakery or cafe website needs:

  1. Menu with photos and prices — The #1 thing customers look for
  2. Location with Google Maps — So people can navigate to you
  3. Phone number and WhatsApp button — For orders and enquiries
  4. Opening hours — Especially holiday hours
  5. Customer reviews/testimonials — Social proof that builds trust
