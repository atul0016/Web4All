FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

What are your operating hours? +
We're open Monday to Saturday, 9:00 AM to 9:00 PM. Sundays by appointment only.
Do you offer home delivery? +
Yes! We offer free delivery within 5km of our store for orders above ₹500. Additional charges apply for farther distances.
Can I return or exchange products? +
We accept returns and exchanges within 7 days of purchase with the original receipt. Products must be in their original condition.
Do you accept online payments? +
Yes, we accept UPI (GPay, PhonePe, Paytm), credit/debit cards, and of course, cash.
How can I contact you for bulk orders? +
For bulk orders, please send us an email at info@example.com. We offer special rates for wholesale buyers.
